About Us

At Ayya Says – Akka Knows, we believe in the magic of storytelling and the importance of preserving the rich cultural traditions that define Sri Lanka. Our mission is to create a lively and vibrant platform that brings to life the wisdom, humor, and beauty of Sri Lankan culture – all while sparking conversations among today's youth.

Our Story

Our Mission

At Ayya Says – Akka Knows, we believe in the power of storytelling and the importance of preserving cultural traditions. Our mission is to create a vibrant platform that celebrates the rich heritage of Sri Lanka, inspiring others to embrace the beauty and wisdom of our island home.

Our Vision

Through our unique blend of content, we aim to forge meaningful connections, foster a deeper understanding of Sri Lankan culture, and empower individuals to embrace their roots. Join us on this journey as we explore the enchanting world of Ayya Says – Akka Knows.

Why We're Different

Our Approach

At Ayya Says - Akka Knows, we take a holistic and thoughtful approach to celebrating Sri Lankan culture. Our team of passionate storytellers, designers, and cultural enthusiasts work tirelessly to create content that is not only visually stunning but also deeply meaningful and educational.
